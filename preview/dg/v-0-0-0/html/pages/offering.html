ISO Consulting

Your Compliance Partner

ISO standards make things work by facilitating business operations and sales and at the same time serve as proof of quality, safety, and efficiency. The legal, industry, customers’, and internal requirements are becoming increasingly complicated, and ISO certification becomes a required element, not optional. By nearly approaching the ISO implementation, an ISO certification will provide great benefit to your organization.

We at Ducis Group aim to help you get it done efficiently and take care of all the routine processes. Within our ISO consulting services, our expert team offers services for Quality, Environment, Work Environment, and Information Security standards that can save time and cost, improve efficiency, and boost customer satisfaction and retention.

Some commonly used ISO Standards are as follows:

  • ISO 9001:2015: Standard for Quality Management System
  • ISO 14001:2015: Standard for Environmental Management System
  • ISO/IEC 19770-1:2017: IT asset management system
  • ISO 20000-1:2018: Management Service management system
  • ISO 21001:2018: Management systems for educational organizations
  • ISO 22301:2012: Business continuity management systems
  • ISO/IEC 27001:2022: Information Security management system
  • ISO 31000:2018: Risk management – Guidelines
  • ISO 45001:2018: Occupational Health & Safety Management System
  • ISO 50001:2011: Energy Management
  • ISO 55001:2014: Asset management – Management systems

If you require consultancy for a Standard not listed above, please contact our team as we may still be able to support you.
